Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scranbled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 1tbs creams

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan ober a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omlette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until serbed)
