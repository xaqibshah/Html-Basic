From Garden to Plate

A potageer is a French term for an omamental vegatable or kitchen garden. Often flowers (adible and non-edible) and herbs are planted with the veetable to enhance the garden's beauty. The goal is to make the function of providing food aesthetically pleasing.

What to Plant

Plants are chosen as much for their functionality at for their color and form. Many are trained to grow upward. A well designed potager can provide foor, out flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintenance. Potahers can disguise their function of providing for a home in a wide array of forms - from the carefree style of the cottage garden to the formally of a knot garden.
